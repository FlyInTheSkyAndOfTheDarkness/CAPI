Privacy Policy — Lead_Researcher

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This application ("Lead_Researcher", "the App") is an internal tool used by its owner to discover publicly available posts on Threads via the official Threads API and forward relevant ones to a private Telegram channel. The App is not offered to third parties and does not provide any public-facing service.

Data we access

Through the official Threads API the App reads publicly available post data returned by keyword search (post text, author username, permalink and timestamp). The App authenticates only with the owner's own Threads account.

How data is used

Retrieved public posts are filtered for relevance and forwarded to a private Telegram channel owned by the operator. Data is used solely for internal lead discovery and is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Data storage and retention

The App stores only identifiers of already-processed posts locally, to avoid sending duplicates. No personal data is stored beyond what is necessary for this de-duplication, and it may be deleted at any time by the operator.

Data sharing

We do not share collected data with any third parties. Data is transmitted only to the operator's own private Telegram channel.

Data deletion

To request deletion of any data related to this App, contact the email below and the locally stored data will be removed.
